About Me

Engineer. Builder.
Robot Whisperer.

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at Nirma University with a deep passion for robotics and autonomous systems. My work lives at the intersection of mechanical design and software — from precise CAD assemblies in SolidWorks to ROS2 navigation stacks running on real competition hardware.

As a two-time national podium finisher at DD Robocon — winning in 2024 and finishing runners-up in 2025 — I've built robots that perform under real pressure. That experience drives me to keep pushing further: I'm currently developing custom path planning algorithms for both differential drive and 3-wheel swerve configurations, going beyond what Nav2 provides out of the box.

I believe in building things that work in the real world, not just in simulation.
