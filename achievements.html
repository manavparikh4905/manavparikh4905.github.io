DD Robocon 2024 · National Competition · Nirma University
National Champions — 1st Place

Secured first place at DD Robocon 2024, representing Nirma University against the strongest engineering teams from across India. The competition, broadcast nationally on DD Sports, is India's most visible robotics event at the undergraduate level.

The result was the product of a full season's work: mechanical design and fabrication in SolidWorks, a complete ROS2 autonomous navigation stack tuned on competition hardware, and extensive field testing under competition-realistic conditions. Every subsystem performed reliably on the day.

First Runners Up — 2nd Place

Returned to the national podium at DD Robocon 2025, finishing as first runners up. Each Robocon season centres on a new international theme — the robot is designed and built entirely from scratch, with different mechanical requirements, field geometry, and task structure.

Back-to-back podium finishes across two entirely different competition themes and robot architectures reflect consistent engineering process, team cohesion, and the ability to execute reliably under pressure — qualities that compound across seasons.
