01
Ongoing · ROS2 · C++
Path Planner — Differential Drive Robot

A from-scratch path planning algorithm for differential drive robots, implemented as a Nav2 plugin. Focuses on smooth trajectory generation, real-time obstacle avoidance, and velocity profiling tuned for non-holonomic constraints. Goes beyond Nav2's default DWB local planner by implementing a custom spline-based trajectory sampler with curvature limits and dynamic lookahead adjustment.

The planner integrates directly with the Nav2 lifecycle and costmap2d infrastructure, allowing seamless drop-in replacement in any standard ROS2 navigation setup.

02
Ongoing · ROS2 · Kinematics
Path Planner — 3-Wheel Omnidirectional (Swerve) Drive

Extending path planning to the kinematically richer 3-wheel omnidirectional swerve configuration — a holonomic platform capable of translating and rotating independently. Involves custom kinematic modeling of the 3WD geometry, holonomic velocity decomposition into individual wheel velocity/angle commands, and heading-independent motion planning.

The planner supports arbitrary heading control decoupled from velocity direction — a key advantage for competition tasks requiring precise positioning while maintaining field orientation.

03
Robocon 2024–2025 · Full Stack Navigation
Robocon Autonomous Navigation Stack

Complete ROS2 autonomous navigation stack built for competition robots at DD Robocon. The stack integrates SLAM Toolbox for real-time map building, AMCL for probabilistic Monte Carlo localization within a known map, and an Extended Kalman Filter for fusing wheel odometry with IMU data into a reliable pose estimate.

Nav2's BehaviorTree-based mission executor handles high-level task sequencing. Tuned and deployed on hardware under competition conditions — contributed to the National Championship win in 2024 and Runners Up finish in 2025.
