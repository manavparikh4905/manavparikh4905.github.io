DD Robocon Championships

DD Robocon (Doordarshan Robocon) is India's premier national robotics competition, part of the Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union Robot Contest. I've competed twice representing Nirma University, achieving back-to-back podium finishes.

Competition Highlights

  • DD Robocon 2024 — 🥇 National Champions. Designed and built both manual and autonomous robots for the "Harvest Day" theme. Full autonomous traversal with object detection and placement.
  • DD Robocon 2025 — 🥈 First Runners Up. Engineered a three-wheeled omnidirectional swerve robot with autonomous path planning for the themed challenge.

My Role

As the navigation and controls lead, I was responsible for:

  • Designing the complete ROS2 navigation pipeline for the autonomous robot
  • Implementing custom path planners optimized for the competition arena's specific obstacles and constraints
  • Integrating LiDAR and camera sensors for real-time environment perception
  • Tuning PID controllers and velocity profiles for precise trajectory following
  • Testing and iterating the autonomous strategy over 100+ field test hours

Engineering Challenges

Competition robotics pushes the limits of rapid engineering. Each season involves 6+ months of design, fabrication, and software development under strict weight, size, and cost constraints. The robots must perform reliably under intense competitive pressure with zero margin for error.
