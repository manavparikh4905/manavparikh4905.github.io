About

Background

Education, research focus, and academic profile.

I am a third-year Mechanical Engineering student at Nirma University, Ahmedabad. My academic focus spans autonomous robotics, robot navigation, and the integration of mechanical design with embedded software systems.

My primary technical work revolves around the ROS2 ecosystem — building and tuning full autonomous navigation stacks using Nav2, SLAM Toolbox, AMCL, and Extended Kalman Filters for sensor fusion. I am currently developing custom path planning algorithms from scratch for both differential drive and 3-wheel omnidirectional swerve drive robots, going beyond what Nav2 provides out of the box.

On the mechanical side, I design robots in SolidWorks — handling full CAD assemblies including chassis, custom drivetrains, and structural subsystems. As a two-time national podium finisher at DD Robocon (2024 National Champions, 2025 Runners Up), I have built and deployed robots under real competition conditions.
